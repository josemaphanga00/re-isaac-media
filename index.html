About

Isaac Media

Isaac Media is a creative visual brand founded by Isaac Ngobeni, a passionate photographer and videographer dedicated to capturing life’s most meaningful moments with authenticity and excellence.

With over 10 years of experience in videography, 4 years in video editing, and 4 years in professional photography, Isaac brings a unique storytelling approach shaped by his journey behind the camera. His foundation in videography influences how he captures still images — focusing not just on how moments look, but how they feel.

Isaac Media specializes in wedding, lifestyle, event, and family photography, creating timeless visuals that reflect genuine emotion, connection, and detail.

Founded in August 2022, the brand was built on a long-standing passion that began at the age of 16 while serving in the church media department. What started as a place of learning quickly grew into a full-time career.

Today, Isaac Media continues to evolve, delivering high-quality visual experiences that not only document moments but preserve stories for generations.
